About us.

Hi. The coding club is an opensource project that centers around community-based learning. We encourage people to contribute to the source code. You can contribute from anywhere and anytime.

Specificity.

Ideally, we want quality but quantity has a quality in of itself. Specifications are the standard but in terms of functionality, it is simply the minimum requirements allowing us to ship what we've made when we are done.

The Timeline.

See how far we have made and what you can do to help us get further.

2017, November

A handful of people started the coding club, then more came. We gathered and the coding club identity was beginning to take shape. Although it might've started sometime in November, nobody really knows when it started.

2017, December

Some sort of activity within the club began to emerge.

2018, January-May

App development, Game development and robotics in the pursuing months.

2018, June

We set up our booths in the following month. Members try to pinch in whatever they have left of their time.

2018, September

New school year, new faces. Some of our graduates have left but we still continue the coding tradition.

People Involved
